# 👁️ Eye-Pulse: Low-Cost IoT Glaucoma Screening

**Eye-Pulse** represents a transformative leap in ophthalmic diagnostics by addressing the critical gap in global eye-care accessibility. This solution is exceptionally effective because it utilizes ultrasound technology to generate **Acoustic Radiation Force (ARF)**, providing a precise yet non-invasive measurement of intraocular pressure (IOP) without the need for anesthetic drops or painful contact.

---

### 🚨 Problem Statement
* 🌍 **Global Challenge**: Glaucoma is one of the leading causes of irreversible blindness globally, impacting millions of individuals and posing a serious public health challenge.
* 📈 **Risk Factor**: Elevated Intraocular pressure (IOP) is the primary risk factor associated with the onset and progression of glaucoma, making early and regular IOP monitoring essential for preventing permanent vision loss.
* 💰 **Current Barriers**: Despite this need, current IOP measurement techniques are often costly, bulky, and dependent on specialized equipment and trained medical professionals.
* 📍 **Inequality**: These limitations significantly restrict access to timely diagnosis, especially in rural and resource-constrained regions, resulting in delayed treatment and a widening gap in equitable eye-care services.

### 💡 Our Solution
* 🛠️ **Low-Cost & Portable**: Our solution is a low-cost, portable, and non-invasive system for measuring intraocular pressure (IOP) using ultrasound technology.
* 🔊 **ARF Technology**: The device uses ultrasonic waves to generate Acoustic Radiation Force (ARF), causing minute deformation of the eye, which is analyzed to estimate IOP.
* 🔌 **IoT Integration**: The system is powered by an ESP8266 microcontroller and connected to an IoT dashboard.
* 📊 **Smart Dashboard**: The dashboard displays the IOP level, risk status, and whether treatment is required.
* 🏥 **Guided Intervention**: It also guides users on the next steps by suggesting nearby hospitals and eye specialists, enabling timely and informed medical intervention.

### 💻 Tech Stack
* 📡 **Ultrasonic Sensor**: Used to generate Acoustic Radiation Force and measure eye deformation.
* 🧠 **ESP8266 Microcontroller**: Handles sensor data processing and IoT connectivity.
* 📝 **Embedded C / Arduino Framework**: For device programming and control logic.
* 🧮 **Signal Processing Algorithms**: For noise reduction, calibration, and accurate measurement.
* ☁️ **IoT Dashboard & Cloud**: Displays IOP level, risk status, and stores patient data for remote access.
