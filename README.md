# Eye-Pulse: Low-Cost IoT Glaucoma Screening

Eye-Pulse is a low-cost, portable, and non-invasive system designed for measuring intraocular pressure (IOP) using ultrasound technology. It addresses the global challenge of irreversible blindness by providing an accessible diagnostic tool for rural and resource-constrained regions.

---

### **Problem Statement**
* Glaucoma is one of the leading causes of irreversible blindness globally, impacting millions of individuals and posing a serious public health challenge.
* Elevated Intraocular pressure (IOP) is the primary risk factor associated with the onset and progression of glaucoma, making early and regular IOP monitoring essential for preventing permanent vision loss.
* Current IOP measurement techniques are often costly, bulky, and dependent on specialized equipment and trained medical professionals.
* These limitations significantly restrict access to timely diagnosis, especially in rural and resource-constrained regions, resulting in delayed treatment and a widening gap in equitable eye-care services.

### **Our Solution**
* Our solution is a low-cost, portable, and non-invasive system for measuring intraocular pressure (IOP) using ultrasound technology. 
* The device uses ultrasonic waves to generate Acoustic Radiation Force (ARF), causing minute deformation of the eye, which is analyzed to estimate IOP.
* The system is powered by an ESP8266 microcontroller and connected to an IoT dashboard.
* The dashboard displays the IOP level, risk status, and whether treatment is required.
* It also guides users on the next steps by suggesting nearby hospitals and eye specialists, enabling timely and informed medical intervention.

### **Tech Stack**
* **Ultrasonic Sensor**: Used to generate Acoustic Radiation Force and measure eye deformation.
* **ESP8266 Microcontroller**: Handles sensor data processing and IoT connectivity.
* **Embedded C / Arduino Framework**: For device programming and control logic.
* **Signal Processing Algorithms**: For noise reduction, calibration, and accurate measurement.
* **IoT Dashboard**: Displays IOP level, risk status, and treatment recommendations.
* **Cloud Platform**: Stores patient data and enables remote access.
