# Eye-Pulse: Low-Cost IoT Glaucoma Screening

[cite_start]Eye-Pulse is a low-cost, portable, and non-invasive system designed for measuring intraocular pressure (IOP) using ultrasound technology[cite: 17]. [cite_start]It addresses the global challenge of irreversible blindness by providing an accessible diagnostic tool for rural and resource-constrained regions[cite: 12, 15].

---

### **Problem Statement**
* [cite_start]Glaucoma is one of the leading causes of irreversible blindness globally, impacting millions of individuals and posing a serious public health challenge[cite: 12].
* [cite_start]Elevated Intraocular pressure (IOP) is the primary risk factor associated with the onset and progression of glaucoma, making early and regular IOP monitoring essential for preventing permanent vision loss[cite: 13].
* [cite_start]Current IOP measurement techniques are often costly, bulky, and dependent on specialized equipment and trained medical professionals[cite: 14].
* [cite_start]These limitations significantly restrict access to timely diagnosis, especially in rural and resource-constrained regions, resulting in delayed treatment and a widening gap in equitable eye-care services[cite: 15].

### **Our Solution**
* [cite_start]Our solution is a low-cost, portable, and non-invasive system for measuring intraocular pressure (IOP) using ultrasound technology[cite: 17]. 
* [cite_start]The device uses ultrasonic waves to generate Acoustic Radiation Force (ARF), causing minute deformation of the eye, which is analyzed to estimate IOP[cite: 18].
* [cite_start]The system is powered by an ESP8266 microcontroller and connected to an IoT dashboard[cite: 19].
* [cite_start]The dashboard displays the IOP level, risk status, and whether treatment is required[cite: 20].
* [cite_start]It also guides users on the next steps by suggesting nearby hospitals and eye specialists, enabling timely and informed medical intervention[cite: 21].

### **Tech Stack**
* [cite_start]**Ultrasonic Sensor**: Used to generate Acoustic Radiation Force and measure eye deformation[cite: 47].
* [cite_start]**ESP8266 Microcontroller**: Handles sensor data processing and IoT connectivity[cite: 47].
* [cite_start]**Embedded C / Arduino Framework**: For device programming and control logic[cite: 47].
* [cite_start]**Signal Processing Algorithms**: For noise reduction, calibration, and accurate measurement[cite: 47].
* [cite_start]**IoT Dashboard**: Displays IOP level, risk status, and treatment recommendations[cite: 47].
* [cite_start]**Cloud Platform**: Stores patient data and enables remote access[cite: 47].
